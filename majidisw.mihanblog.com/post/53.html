See below for upcoming chat topics  in February and March


  Sunday    February 13th, 2011     9:00 P.M. - 10:00 P.M "A Love Letter to Social Work" 

 

Join your social work colleagues for a special pre-Valentine's Day Chat, we're calling "A Love Letter to Social Work"...

Try your own... (fill in the blanks..)

 Dear Social Work,

Ever since we met I've __________

Our relationship has been ________

I knew it was love when ________

 Listen to colleagues profess their love for their profession... (Caveat:  Like much love... ours is complicated...expect to hear a mixed bag of emotions)... We look forward to a lively discussion.

 

Tuesday    February 15th, 2011    9:00 P.M. - 10:00 P.M Creativity & the Arts in Social Work Counseling 

 

Take a  look at the use of poetry, journaling, bibliotherapy and art as tools to enhance your social work counseling skills. Mila Tecala and Camielle Call will host on Tuesday, February 15th, from 9 - 10 PM EST

 

 Sunday    February 20th, 2011     9:00 P.M. - 10:00 P.M  Licensure Preparation

 

Join your social work colleagues to discuss the finer points of taking and passing the social work licensure examination.   I will lead the discussion using tips and tricks from the licensure prep course I teach - Kevin Schablein will also host. 

 

 Tuesday    February 22nd, 2011    9:00 P.M. - 10:00 P.M Psychological Impact/Serious Medical Illness 

 

On this night we'll take a closer look at the psychological impact of being diagnosed with and treated for a serious medical illness. Share some of the work you are doing to assist those living with these illnesses, learn more about how social workers can help.  Camielle Call and Mila Tecala will lead the discussion
